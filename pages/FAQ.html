Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any requirements for adopting dogs?

Answer: Just a few!

Although we would love to be able to give everyone who is willing the opportunity to adopt, some requirements must first be met.

  • Must be 18 years or older, or have the consent of a parent or guardian before adopting.
  • Identification, such as a license or passport, must be presented at the time of adoption.
  • Adopters must be able to provide a safe and stable living environment for their newfound family.
  • Adopters must not have any violent criminal records, such as a history of abuse or assault.

Find out if one of our puts is the right fit for you on our Adoption page!

How much does it cost to adopt a dog?

Answer: 20$

Only a small service fee of 20$ is required to adopt a furry friend. This is in part thanks to our generous donators who ensure that we can keep prices low for families who wish to adopt.

Can I come/bring my kids to play with the dogs?

Answer: Absolutley!

We love when families come and interact with the pups! You can call ahead and schedule an appointment to come play with them over the phone or on our Volunteer page in just a few minutes. And of course, it is completely free!

Are your dogs vaccinated?

Answer: Yes!

All of our dogs are vaccinated and up to date on their shots. We also make sure to give them regular checkups to ensure that they are healthy and happy!

Can I foster a dog before deciding to adopt?

Answer: Yes!

We understand that adopting can be a big decision, and we encourage families who are unsure about the commitment to foster one of our pups before adopting! If you do decide to adopt, just stop by to finish some paperwork give your fostered pup a permenant home.
