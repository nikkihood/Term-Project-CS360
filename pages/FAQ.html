Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any requirements for adopting dogs?

Answer: Yes

Although we would love to be able to give everyone who is willing the opportunity to adopt, some requirements must first be met.

  • Must be 18 years or older, or have the consent of a parent or guardian before adopting.
  • Identification, such as a license or passport, must be presented at the time of adoption.
  • Adopters must be able to provide a safe and stable living environment for their newfound family.
  • Adopters must not have any violent criminal records, such as a history of abuse or assault.
How much does it cost to adopt a dog?

Answer: 20$

Only a small service fee of 20$ is required to adopt a furry friend. This is in part thanks to our generous donators who ensure that we can keep prices low for families who wish to adopt.
