News Articles

Dog On the Loose

4 year old pug named Missy is currently missing. Owners say she was last seen by her home near the University of jacksonville 3 days ago. If you see any pug with a pink collar and chipped ear, please call our shelter or the owners to help her reach her family that are worried about her.

Celebrating National Adopt a Shelter Pet Day

On April 30th, we are celebrating national adopt a shelter pet day! At all of our locations alongside other shelters will be doing fun activities to celebrate this event. This includes letting you pet all of our pups and giving discounts for adoptions. Feel free to join us at any location since this is a holiday that you should not miss!

Local Dog Saver

Recently, on the 14th of April, a dog owner accidently lost their pup and it was later found towards the top of a decently high tree. A concerned local firefighter saw the owner worried about her pup and the local firefighter decided to climb the tree and rescure the pup. Luckily no pup or person was injured and the owner thanked the firefighter numerous times.
